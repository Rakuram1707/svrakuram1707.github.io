IND VS NZ

India wins by 70 runs

Day of records for Indian cricket. First it was Virat Kohli, who scored a record-breaking 50th ODI hundred.
The rest of the bating unit, starting with Rohit up top and Shreyas in the death, fired in unison as India posted a mammoth 397.
New Zealand kept the fight going for as long they could. Darryl Mitchell scored another chanceless hundred, his second against India in the tournament.
Williamson and Phillips played handy knocks. But once Shami got into his groove, there was no stopping India.
The right-arm pacer secured a seven-wicket haul, a first-ever by an Indian, to help India to a 70-run win.
Now, the final awaits!!
